Our Story

SavingsTarget was founded with one simple mission: to help individuals and families plan and achieve their financial goals. Our journey started with the belief that everyone deserves access to tools that make saving easier.

We noticed a gap in the market for user-friendly financial tools that could guide people through their savings journey. So, we created SavingsTarget, where anyone can easily calculate how much they need to save to reach their goals, whether it's buying a car, a house, or something else entirely.

Today, SavingsTarget is trusted by thousands of users. We continue to expand our features, providing the best financial resources and tips to ensure our users have everything they need to succeed.
